Built on a Zero-Trust Architecture

At DevoCare, we believe security isn't just a feature; it's a fundamental right. Our systems are built from the ground up to ensure that your medical history, personal details, and clinical data are accessible only by you and the professionals you authorize.

We use end-to-end encryption for all sensitive data movement and storage, ensuring that even in the unlikely event of a breach, your data remains unintelligible.

256-bit AES Encryption

Industry-standard encryption that secures every byte of data while it rests on our servers and while it travels over the web.

HIPAA & ABDM Compliant

Fully aligned with global and local healthcare standards, including the Digital Health Stack requirements for India.

Anonymized Analytics

We strip all personal identifiers before performing any clinical analytics, ensuring your privacy is never compromised for data insights.
